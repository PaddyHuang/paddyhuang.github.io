何凯文考研词汇 1~20

1. lucrative

  • adj. = handsome

    eg.
    lucrative(handsome) reward 诱人的奖赏，丰厚的奖赏

  • adj. = profitable

    eg.
    lucrative(profitable) product 诱人的商品，能赚取利润的商品

  • adj. = rich

    eg.
    lucrative(rich) guy 充满诱惑的男人，有钱的男人

2. susceptible

  • adj.
  • A is susceptible to B (A易受B的影响)
    = is vulnerable to B
  • A is susceptibel to doing sth. (A很可能做某事)
    = is prone to do sth.
    = is likely to do sth.
    = is liable to do sth.
  • A is susceptible (A多愁善感)
    = is sensitive
    = is sentimental

考研最长的词：Pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis 火山岩尘肺病

3. weather

  • n. 天气
  • v. 克服
  • weather the crisis 渡过危机
  • weather the setback 克服困境

success and setback 成功和困境 (never say failure)

4. The Court

  • n. 联邦最高法院(美)
  • = The Federal supreme Court

supreme = super + me( = god)
由于元音辅音凑音节的需要，更换字母的位置

  • Justies n. 最高法院法官

    最高法院法官(9个人：8个大法官 + 1个chief)
    只有当8个大法官投票成4:4时，chief首席大法官才参与，故若5:4，chief就参加了

justice and freedom 常搭配在一起

5. The House

  • n. 众议院

    450+席位

  • The Senate n. 参议院

    101个席位

  • The Congress n. 国会 = The House + The Senate
  • The White House n. 白宫

6. check and balance

  • 制衡，三权分立
  • check
    • v. 检查
    • v. check = limit 限制

    eg.
    unchecked power 不受限制的权利
    This adverse trend should be checked. 有害的潮流应该被限制

7. 单词记忆方法

  • turbulent adj. 混乱的 -> 特别乱的
  • agony adj. 痛苦的 -> 爱过你

8. say

  • conj. 比如

    eg.

    1. Investment – in railways, say, or in new housing – increase the size of market.
      比如，在铁路方面或新的房地产项目中的投资会增加市场的规模。
    2. The novelists, say Dickens, have influenced the history of England.
      许多小说家，例如狄更斯，对于英国的历史产生了影响。
  • v. 说

    eg.
    The English learning, says Kevin, is very important.
    Kevin说，英语学习很重要。

9. similar

  • adj. 相似
  • assimilate v. 使同化
  • assimilating adj. 同化的

    eg.
    assimilating power 同化的力量
    = soft power 软实力
    = common culture + popular culture
    = 大众文化 + 流行文化

  • homo- 同-

    homo-sex 同性

10. perform

  • performance n. 性能/表现

    eg.

    1. A mobile phone can perform many tasks at once.
      一台手机能同时运行多个任务。
    2. perform miracles 创造奇迹
    3. perfect storm 惊涛骇浪

11. patriot

  • n. 爱国者
  • patriotic adj. 爱国的
  • patriotism n. 爱国主义

12. nostalgia

  • n. 乡愁，怀旧

    eg.

    1. When I was a child, nostalgia seemed a small stamp. Here am i. And there my mom.
      小时候，乡愁是一枚小小的邮票。我在这头，妈妈在那头。
    2. nostalgia for the ink
      对油墨香味的怀旧

13. drum

  • n. 鼓
  • v. 鼓动
  • drum up sb. to do sth. 鼓动某人去做某事

    eg.
    If you want to build a ship, don’t drum up people to collect wood and dont’t assign them tasks or work, but rather teach them long for the endless immensity of the sea.
    如果你想造一艘船，不用鼓动人们收集木头，也不用给他们布置任务或工作，相反，去告诉他们对无穷浩瀚大海的向往。

14. triumph

  • n. 胜利，凯旋
  • Triumph n. 凯旋门

15. entitle

  • adj. 做后置定语，名叫…

    eg.
    A book entitled The old man and the sea, drumps up me to strive and to seek the true meaning of life.
    一本名叫老人与海的书一直鼓励我奋斗，并追寻生命的意义。

  • 搭配：entitle sb. (to) sth. 使某人具有sth.

    eg.
    The language ability entitles you (to) the competitive edge of the future job market.
    语言能力让你具有在未来工作市场上的竞争优势。

  • 搭配：sb. be entitled to do sth. 有权利做某事

    eg.

    1. Everyone is entitled to their own opion.
      每个人都有表达自我观点的权利。
    2. I disagree with what you say, but I will defend [to the death] your right to say it.
      我不同意你所说的，但我坚决捍卫你说话的权利。

16. eligible

  • adj. 有资格的
  • 搭配：be elegible for/to do sth.

    eg.
    Those who are not upfront are not eligible for the benefits.
    那些不诚实的人，没有资格获得福利。
    upfront = honest = frank = upstraight adj. 正直，诚实，坦诚，率真

17. aspire

  • v. 渴望 = long for
  • 搭配：aspire to sth. 想要获得sth.
  • 搭配：aspire to do sth. = long for doing sth. 想要去做sth.

    eg.

    1. Aspire to inspire until I expire. (押韵)
      生命不息，奋斗不止。
    2. boom and bust
      兴衰
    3. introduce and enforce
      推行和实施
    4. survive and thrive
      活下去，枝繁叶茂
    5. success and setback
      成功与挫折

18. legitimate

  • adj. 合法的
  • adj. 合理的

    eg.
    legitimate research 合理的研究

legal
illegal

19. dread

  • n. 恐惧
  • dreadful adj. 恐怖的

    eg.
    dreadful headline 令人恐惧的报道
    nasty headline 负面报道

考研英语常来自media English 媒体英语
main stream 主流 = media English
《The Economist》 《经济学人》
《The Scientific American》 《科学美国人》
《The US News & World report》
Ranking of the University(US) 美国大学排名
《The Wall Street Journal》 《华尔街日报》
The high street -> 特指伦敦商业街
The fleet street -> 英国舰队街(小报出版社)
《The Harvard Business Review》 《哈佛商业评论》

20. dismiss

  • 搭配：dismiss A as B 认为A是B而不予考虑(理会)

    eg.

    1. Donald Trump dismissed the option poll as worthless.
      唐纳德 特朗普认为这个民意调查是没有价值的。
      fake news 虚假新闻
    2. Fake it, make it!

与dismiss相似的搭配：

  1. accept A as B
  2. view A as B
  3. treat A as B
    eg.
    He did not accept as well-funded the charge made by his critics.
    他不认为他的批评者所提出的指责是有根据的。
  • well-funded adj. 有根据的
  • charge n. 指控，指责
  • critic n. 批评者，批判者 (critics)
  • criticism n. 批评

小结

  2. 必考搭配
  3. 必考替换

何凯文考研词汇 21~50

21. suspect

  • v. n. 怀疑
  • doubt v. n.
    • suspect + n. = doubt + n. 怀疑
    • suspect + 句子 = believe + 句子 认为
    • doubt + 句子 = deny + 句子 = don’t believe + 句子 否认

22. compare

  • compare v. 比较
  • comparison n. 比较相同点
  • contrast v.
  • contrast n. 比较不同点

[Note] compare 既可以找出相同点，也可以找出不同点

23. patchwork

  • n. 各自为政(中性偏贬义)
  • patchwork regulation
  • patchwork mess 由于各自为政引起的混乱

State government 州政府
Federal government 联邦政府 -> clear-cut regulation 一刀切政策 -> central authority 中央集权
The Constitution 宪法

24. possible

  • adj. 可能的
  • 同义替换 looming

    eg.
    looming danger = possible dange 可能的，潜在的危险

25. asset

  • n. 财产，品质

    eg.
    The decisiveness and patience are the invaluable asset to one’s success.
    决心和耐心是一个人成功的重要因素。

26. conscious

  • adj. 有意识的
  • 反义词：unconscious adj. = without realizing sth.没意识的

27. forge

  • v. 铸就
  • v. 伪造

    eg.
    The honesty forges the supporting structure of our society.
    诚信铸造我们社会的支撑结构。
    filial duty 孝道
    filial piety
    filial respect

28. margin

  • n. 空白(边)
  • n. 利润空间(经济学) = profit

    eg.
    keep mental and physical fit
    保持身心健康
    The margin has been squeezed.
    利润空间被压缩。

29. emerge

  • emerging adj. 新兴的

    eg.
    emerging market 新兴市场
    emerginh economy 新兴经济

30. dynamic

  • adj. 动态的 = changeable
  • 反义词：mechanical adj. 机械的，呆板的

    eg.
    mechanical learning 机械读书，读死书
    = unchangeable learning

31. halt

  • v. n. 停止 = stop = end = phase out(逐步停止)

    eg.
    come to halt = discontinue 停止

  • halt + 规定/法律/做法

    eg.
    halt the practise of …
    停止。。。的做法

32. plausible

  • adj. 花言巧语的
  • adj. 合理的 = reasonable

33. viable

  • adj. 可行的

    eg.
    His proposal is not only plausible but viable

34. default

  • adj. 默认的

    eg.
    default setting 默认设置

35. envy

  • v. n. 羡慕
  • enviable adj. 别人羡慕你

    eg.

    1. Your effort entitled you to this enviable position.
      你的努力使你拥有了这个令人羡慕的职位。
  • envious adj. 你羡慕别人

    eg.

    1. Western nations are envious of our country’s productivity and sicial harmony.
      西方国家羡慕我们国家的生产率和社会和谐度。

36. grill

  • v. n. 烧烤

  • grill the idea 反复地思考

  • grill the plan
    = think it over

    eg.
    Bar and Grill 小酒和简餐(小店门口)

37. temper

  • n. 脾气
  • v. 控制 = control

    eg.

    1. temper/control the emotion
      控制脾气
    2. temper/control the speeding
      控制花费
  • temple n. 庙

    戒定慧

38. modify

  • v. 改变，改善 = change

39. evolution

  • n. 进化
  • n. 过程 = process

40. preside

  • v. 主持

  • 搭配：preside over 做出…，主持…

    eg.
    preside over the meeting 主持会议
    preside over the decision 做出决定

  • president n. 总统，校长

41. overlook

  • v. 忽视
  • overestimate v. 高估 = overrate
  • oversee v. 监管

42. curb and harness

  • = control = rein in 驾驭，控制

43. novel

  • n. 小说
  • adj. = new = nascent = emerging
    • nov- = new = na-

      innovation 创新
      lenovo (Legand)
      nascent industry 新型产业

44. pore

  • n. 毛孔
  • 搭配：pore over = research 仔细研究

45. sober

  • v. 唤醒
  • 搭配：sober sb. up 唤醒某人
  • sobering adj. 清醒的

    the mangement of time
    eg.
    The bad news has a sobering effects on all of us.
    坏消息总能给我们警醒作用。

46. assert

  • v. 主张，认为 = contend = argue = think
  • assertiveness n. 信心，自信 = faith = confidence

47. encourage

  • adj. 因果词

    eg.
    The sorry state of affairs is partly encouraged by the lack of corresponding laws and regulations.
    这个遗憾局面部分是由缺乏相应的法律和法规造成的。

48. stark

  • adj. 荒芜
  • the stark fact that 明显，毫无遮掩的
  • stark mad 彻底疯了

49. rail

  • 搭配：rail against 反对

50. curtail

  • v. = cut 削减，裁剪

    eg.

    1. curtail the budget 削减预算
    2. The plan reflects the curtailed ambition off the EU.
      计划反映了欧盟信心不足。(这个计划就是欧盟壮志未酬的体现)
  • tailor n. 裁缝

何凯文考研词汇 51~70

51. champion

  • n. 冠军
  • v. 推崇，主张

    eg.
    The big idea championed by the tech industry is innovation.
    科技行业所推崇的重要理念是创新。

52. environmental protection

  • = environmental preservation 环保
  • green group 环保组织
  • sustainable adj. 可持续的
  • restriction adj. 限制的-> FBI Warning
  • sharing economy 共享经济

53. industriousness and thrift

  • industriousness n. 勤劳 = hardworking
  • thrift n. 节俭
  • industrious and insightful person 勤劳，有洞察力的人

54. approach

  • n. 方法

    eg.
    to make approaches to sb./sth. 主动与某人/某机构接洽

  • n. 通道
  • n. 靠近

    eg.
    the approach of spring 春天的临近

  • v. 靠近，接近

    eg.
    night is approaching.
    夜幕降临。

55. game

  • n. 猎物

    eg.

    1. easy game 容易猎杀的猎物
    2. relentless attack
      relentless adj. 1. 无休止的；2. 残忍的
    3. extinct v. 灭绝
      extinction n. 灭绝
    4. human being 人(包括原始人)
      = Homo Sapiens 现代智人

56. greedy

  • adj. 贪婪 = acquisitive
  • acquire v. 获得
  • greed n. 贪婪

The Seven = original sin = The Seven deadly sins 七宗罪

  1. lust n. 色欲
  2. gluttony n. 暴食
  3. greed n. 贪婪
  4. sloth n. 懒惰
  5. wrath(anger) n. 暴怒
  6. envy n. 嫉妒
  7. arrogence(pride) n. 傲慢

The Seven Virtues

  1. chastity n. 贞洁
  2. temperance n. 节制
  3. charity n. 慷慨
  4. diligence n. 勤勉
  5. patience n. 耐心
  6. kidness n. 宽容
  7. humility n. 谦逊

eg.
Vanity is constant.
虚荣是常量。(人人都爱虚荣)

57. decent

  • adj. 体面的
  • 反义词：vulgar adj. 粗鄙的
  • decency n. 体面 = courtesy

58. courtesy

  • n. 礼数，礼，绅士风度

    eg.
    As a courtesy to the next passenger, may we suggest that you wipe off the wash basin.
    作为对下一个乘客的礼貌，我们建议您把洗手台擦干。

59. cause

  • n. 原因
  • n. 事业

    the great cause 伟大事业

60. exceptional

  • except v. 除外
  • exception n. 例外
  • exceptional adj. 例外的
  • exceptional adj. 非常好的 = briliant = great = successful = well-accepted(注意语境)

    eg.
    the exceptional children 智障儿童

  • exceptionalism n. (美国)例外论

    selected person = the salt of the earth = elite 精英

61. knowledge

  • n. 知识

eg.
Knowledge is power.
知识就是力量。
vision 眼界
insight 洞察力
critical thinking 批判性思维
enthusiasm 激情
ambition 愿景

  • n. 了解，知道

    eg.
    The consuming habits of consumers have been recorded by the browers without their knowledge.
    在消费者不知情的情况下，浏览器记录下了他们的消费习惯。

62. spell

  • v. 拼写
  • n. 咒语
  • v. 导致
  • n. 一段时间

    eg.
    The optimistic spell of 1960s has witness the passion of people on the artificial intelligence(AI).
    乐观的六十年代见证了人们对人工智能的机器
    Curse or bless? 诅咒或福报？
    Bill Gates, Elon Musk, Stephen Hawking -> Curse
    Elloit Zuckerberg -> Bless
    Jack Ma 马云

63. blame

  • 搭配：A is to blame for B (A是原因) A造成了B

    eg.

    1. The widespread use of disposable plastic bags is to blame for environmental damaging/damage.
      塑料袋的广泛使用是造成环境破解的原因。
      disposable adj. 一次性的
    2. That is not to say that mobile phone alone is to blame for the sorry state of affairs.
      并不是说，仅仅因为手机就造成了现在糟糕的局面。

64. cover

  • n. 封面

    cover story 封面故事

  • v. 报道
  • v. 遮盖
  • v. 支付

65. buy

  • v. 相信 = believe

    eg.
    I don’t buy it. = I don’t believe it.
    我不相信/我不买账。

66. literature

  • n. 文学
  • n. 文字材料

    eg.
    distribute the literature 分发资料

67. abuzz

  • eg.
    A move has intellectual-property lawyers abuzz.
    一个行动让/引起，知识产权律师躁动/广泛讨论。
    rule 1. n. 规定；2. n. 判决 = ruling(正式)
    patent n. 专利
    patented product 受到专利保护的产品
    review patent 审查专利

68. price & school & score

  • price -> pricing 定价
  • school -> schooling 学校教育
  • score -> scoring 评分

69. procedure

  • n. 过程
  • n. 手段
  • surgical procedure 外科手术
  • medical procedure = medical initiative(手段) 药物手段

70. resort

  • n. 手段

    eg.
    the best resort 最好的手段
    the last resort 最后的手段

  • v.
  • resort to = turn to 求助于

何凯文考研词汇 71~100

71. help

  • = material support
  • = financial aid
  • = mental comfort
  • = spritual backup

72. succeed

  • = accmulate wealth
  • = establish career
  • = fit into society and even attain social status

73. horizon

  • n. 眼界

    eg.

    1. broaden our horizon 拓宽眼界
    2. There is looming trouble on the horizon.
      = There is looming trouble likely to happen soon.

74. alert

  • n. 警惕

    on the alert 保持警惕 (be prepare)

75. instead

  • adv. 然而 = however 句间转折关系

    eg.

    1. He was ill so I went instead.
      = He was ill so I went in the place of him.
      他病了，所以我去了。
    2. He didm’t reply. Instead, he left the house quickly.
  • instead fo = not 句中否定关系

    eg.
    I can walk to walk instead of going by car.

76. threat

  • treat to sth. (sth.正面)

    threat to your health

  • threat of sth. (sth.负面)

    threat of smoking

  • increase to 增加到

  • increase by 增加了

77. object

  • n. 物体

  • adj. 反对

  • n. 宾语

  • objective adj. 客观的 = impartial 公平的

  • 反义词：subjective adj. 主观的 = partial 偏袒的

  • neutral adj. 中立的，没态度的，漠不关心的
    = indifferent = detached

  • skeptical adj. 怀疑的，表示质疑(反对含义) 近似disagree

  • uncertain adj. = 无态度的

  • 态度题 2～4分

78. The future has arrived. It commences now.

  • 未来已经到来。它已经开始。
  • commence v. 开始

79. liable

  • adj. 可能的
  • A is liable to do sth. A可能去做某事

    eg.
    We are all liable to make mistakes when we are tired.

  • adj. 负责任
  • A is liable for sth. A负…责任
  • adj. 有责任

    eg.
    I’ll be liable if you have an accident.

80. shrink

  • v. 缩小
  • shrink the generation gap 缩小代沟
  • n. 心理医生
  • formal express -> 显得没有生命力
  • informal express -> vivid, lively

    pink slip 解雇通知单
    safety net 安全网, 在social security中表示社保
    parachute n. 降落伞 -> primary earner 主要挣钱, partner -> wife(parachute)
    middle class 中产家庭
    If primary earner got a pink slip(laid off, jobless), the partner will go for a job as a parachute, adding husband’s safety net, which support the family.
    budget to the limit 预算做到极致
    变成 -> double-income family 双收入家庭产生

  • time management 时间管理
  • risk management 风险管理

81. side with

  • 支持

82. aside

  • 搭配：n. + aside, … 除开…

    eg.
    The decrease if Internet-access fee aside, the wide use of smart phone encourages the boom of online education.
    除开互联网费用的降低，智能手机的广泛使用促进了在线教育的繁荣。

83. fair play

  • 公平竞争

84. keep abreast of

  • 了解

85. other than

eg.

  1. I am the best teacher.
    = No one(上义词) other than I(下义词) is the best teacher.
  2. in China
    = In no country other than China,
  • 上义词/下义词 -> 种属词 formal
  • A of B
  • The city(上义词) of Nanning(下义词)
  • The asset of honesty

86. Tit for tat

  • 针锋相对

87. enable

  • = entail = suit 使…能

88. precision

  • n. 准确 = accuracy

90. determine

  • = find out

91. devoid of

  • = lack of = dearth of 缺乏(没有)

92. tap

  • v. 敲打
  • tap the market = explore the market = expand the market 拓展，开发

93. nature

  • adj. 自然的
  • adj. 先天的 = born = intuitive = innate = acquired
  • nurture adj. 后天的 = made = cognitive

    US -> mainstream -> nature

94. apprehension

  • n. 理解

    apprehensive ability 理解能力

  • n. = worry

95. imagine

  • n. 想象
  • n. 认为 = conceive = envision = argue = contend

96. real estate

  • n. 房产 = property 财产

97. prevent

  • v. 阻碍 = hinder = refrain = deter

98. drug

  • n. 药品 = medicine
  • n. 毒品

99. chronic

  • adj. 慢性的 = durable
  • frequent = repeated adj. 反复的

100. cause

  • v. 导致 = lead to = result in = render = make = spur = spark = stimulate = motivate = prompt = inspire = entail = underlie = fuel = push
  • 同义替换
