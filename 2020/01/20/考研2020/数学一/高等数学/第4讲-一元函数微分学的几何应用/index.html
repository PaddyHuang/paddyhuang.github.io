(1) 若f(x)f(x)f(x)在点x=a(a≠0)x=a(a\neq0)x=a(a​=0)处去得极值，试证明其为极小值。
(2) 若f(x)f(x)f(x)在点x=0x=0x=0处取得极值。试问它是极大值还是极小值。
[解]
(1)

由题知，f′(a)=0,代入，得，xf′′(x)+3x[f′(x)2]=1−e−xaf′′(a)=1−e−af′′(a)=1−e−aa=ea−1eaaI. 当a>0,f′′(a)>0II. 当a<0,f′′(a)>0∴f′′(a)>0,(a≠0)即f(x)在x=a处取得极小值。\begin{aligned} & \text{由题知，}f'(a)=0,\\ & \begin{aligned} \text{代入，得，}\quad xf''(x)+3x[f'(x)^2] & = 1-e^{-x}\\ af''(a) & = 1-e^{-a}\\ f''(a) & = \frac{1-e^{-a}}{a} = \frac{e^a-1}{e^aa} \end{aligned}\\ & I.\ \text{当}a>0, f''(a)>0\\ & II.\ \text{当}a<0, f''(a)>0\\ & \therefore f''(a)>0,(a\neq0)\\ & \text{即}f(x)\text{在x=a处取得极小值。} \end{aligned} ​由题知，f′(a)=0,代入，得，xf′′(x)+3x[f′(x)2]af′′(a)f′′(a)​=1−e−x=1−e−a=a1−e−a​=eaaea−1​​I. 当a>0,f′′(a)>0II. 当a<0,f′′(a)>0∴f′′(a)>0,(a​=0)即f(x)在x=a处取得极小值。​

(2)

由题知，f′(0)=0,lim⁡x→0+f′′(x)=lim⁡x→0−f′′(x)=lim⁡x→01−e−xx=lim⁡x→0ex−1exx=1>0∴f(x)在x=0处取得极小值。\begin{aligned} & \text{由题知，}f'(0)=0,\\ & \lim_{x\to0^+}{f''(x)}=\lim_{x\to0^-}{f''(x)}=\lim_{x\to0}{\frac{1-e^{-x}}{x}}=\lim_{x\to0}{\frac{e^{x}-1}{e^xx}}=1>0\\ & \therefore f(x)\text{在x=0处取得极小值。} \end{aligned} ​由题知，f′(0)=0,x→0+lim​f′′(x)=x→0−lim​f′′(x)=x→0lim​x1−e−x​=x→0lim​exxex−1​=1>0∴f(x)在x=0处取得极小值。​
